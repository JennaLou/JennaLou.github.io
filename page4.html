This project was really challenging because I had to do a lot of research and thinkinng about what design I should do. Then, I put 5 cotton balls inside to kind of support the egg when it reaches the impact. So when we put the egg inside the cone I had to glue the plywood on top of the cone so when it drops the egg wouldn't bounce back.
